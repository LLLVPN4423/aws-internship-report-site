### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment** The working environment during the First Cloud AI Journey (FCAJ) program was incredibly friendly and open. Everyone was willing to help whenever I encountered difficulties, especially during late-night debugging sessions for cloud deployments. The workspace, whether remote or at the Bitexco office, was professional and helped me stay focused.
However, because there were so many interns participating in the program, I felt a little disappointed that I couldn't join more of the events organized by the admins. The Community Day sessions were highly valuable, providing practical cloud engineering insights and soft skills shared by experienced IT professionals. 

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin** My mentor provided detailed guidance on AWS architecture and API design, explaining complex concepts clearly whenever I hit a roadblock. I am especially grateful to my mentor for allowing me to explore solutions independently—such as troubleshooting IAM permissions and migrating AWS accounts—instead of simply providing the answers. The admin team also offered excellent support with administrative procedures and documentation. Their constructive feedback contributed significantly to the progress of the GreenLens project.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major** The backend developer tasks assigned to me were directly related to the software engineering curriculum at HUTECH. Building the backend for the GreenLens project, managing DynamoDB, and designing RESTful APIs with FastAPI allowed me to strengthen my academic foundation while developing practical skills. It bridged the gap between university theories and real-world cloud infrastructure.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities** During my internship, I learned numerous technical skills, including Docker containerization, AWS cloud management, and system architecture. Beyond coding, I significantly improved my technical writing by authoring project blogs. I also learned how to collaborate effectively in a cross-functional team, specifically supporting the Frontend team by mapping JSON data, handling error responses, and defining API payloads without needing to write the UI code myself.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit** The company culture was highly positive and collaborative. Everyone respected one another's roles. Whenever we faced integration issues between the Backend and Frontend, we worked together to trace the logs and find the root bugs rather than pointing fingers. Even as an intern, my architectural decisions for the APIs were respected, which made me feel like a truly valued member of the team.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits** The program provided flexible working arrangements and necessary AWS resources. Having the opportunity to participate in internal training sessions and earn the AWS Cloud Practitioner certification was a significant benefit that greatly enhanced my professional profile.

### Additional Questions
- What did you find **most satisfying** during your internship?  
The most satisfying aspect was successfully integrating the complex AI Camera features and seeing the Backend and Frontend connect flawlessly in real-time. Additionally, the energy and dedication of the FCAJ community, mentors, and fellow interns created an inspiring environment that kept me motivated through challenging bug-fixing phases.
- What do you think the company **should improve** for future interns?  
I believe the program could benefit from organizing more technical workshops focusing specifically on cross-functional integration (Backend-Frontend alignment) and cloud resource management (like handling Free Tier credits efficiently). Hosting these on weekdays could provide more flexibility for students.
- If recommending to a friend, would you **suggest they intern here**? Why or why not?  
Absolutely. I would highly recommend this internship program to my peers in the IT sector. It provides hands-on experience with modern cloud technologies, outstanding mentorship, and an inspiring atmosphere that pushes you to grow both technically and professionally.

### Suggestions & Expectations
**Would you like to continue this program in the future?**
Yes. If given the opportunity, I would be thrilled to continue participating in this program. It has been a foundational milestone in my journey as a Backend Developer, equipping me with the exact skills I need for my future career.
