**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [AWS Free Tier Setup & SimuLearn Foundation Labs](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [Advanced SimuLearn Labs: Security, Scaling & VPCs](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [Project Topic Finalization & AWS Cloud Practitioner Certification](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [Local Cloud Environment Setup & Docker/FastAPI Architecture](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [API Initialization, Data Schema & DynamoDB Profile Integration](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [XP Core Logic Development & Advanced DynamoDB Integration](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [API Debugging, State Sync & Frontend Integration Testing](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [AWS Account Migration, IAM Setup & Infrastructure Troubleshooting](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [AI Camera API Integration, S3 Mapping & Mascot Web Speech](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [Technical Documentation, Project Blogs Publishing & Consultation](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [Internship Report Compilation, Proposal & Workshop Rehearsal](1.11-week11/)
